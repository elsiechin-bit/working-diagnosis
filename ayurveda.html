आयुर्वेद  Materia Medica

Interactive Compendium of Ayurvedic Medicines

A clinician-facing reference integrating traditional Ayurvedic classification with modern pharmacology and clinical evidence. Evidence tiers reflect quality of human RCT data.

Clinical disclaimer. This tool is for practitioner reference only -- not for patient self-diagnosis or self-treatment.
  • Heavy metal contamination is a significant safety concern in Ayurvedic preparations. Always use products from reputable manufacturers with third-party batch testing for heavy metals.
  • Rasashastra preparations (metals, minerals) require specialist Ayurvedic supervision. Do not substitute with unverified products.
  • Many Ayurvedic herbs have clinically significant drug interactions (especially via CYP3A4/P-gp). Always take a complete herbal medicine history.
  • Evidence ratings reflect RCT quality for the specific preparation and dose studied -- traditional formulations may differ significantly from studied extracts.
