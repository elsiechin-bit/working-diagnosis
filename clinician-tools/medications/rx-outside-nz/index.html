Prescriptions Outside NZ

Medicines commonly prescribed overseas that are unavailable, unfunded, or differently restricted in NZ. Look up a drug by name or brand to find the NZ status and nearest equivalent.

Always verify current PHARMAC funding status at pharmac.govt.nz and NZF monograph at nzf.org.nz before prescribing. Funding decisions change. Confidence ratings reflect the author's certainty at time of writing, not clinical evidence quality.

No entries match your search. Try a generic name, brand name, or clear the filters.

Data current as of May 2026. Sources: PHARMAC Schedule, NZF, PBS (Australia), NHS (UK), FDA (US). This tool is for GP consultation support - not for patient use. Entries with a moderate confidence badge should be verified against current PHARMAC documentation before acting on them.
