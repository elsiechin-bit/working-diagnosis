Mental health is one of the heaviest workloads in general practice. This pillar covers depression, anxiety, addiction, perinatal mental health, psychosis and bipolar disorder, and the particular shape of youth mental health in Aotearoa. The focus is on what the GP can do in 15 minutes plus what the system around the consult is meant to do.
