The less structured end of the building. Three threads mostly.

**Patient explainers.** Visual aids, animations, and small interactive pieces designed for the consulting room or for the patient to take away. Built when a static handout doesn't quite get there.

**Off-centre builds.** Sketches and tools that didn't fit neatly into the workbench. Some are useful, some are just thinking out loud in code.

**[Field notes.](/field-notes/)** Longform essays and clinical reflections from a working GP. The thinking that doesn't belong in a guideline.
