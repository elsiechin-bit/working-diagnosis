The less structured end of the building. Two threads mostly.

**Patient explainers.** Visual aids, animations, and small interactive pieces designed for the consulting room or for the patient to take away. Built when a static handout doesn't quite get there.

**Off-centre builds.** Sketches, longform essays about working in general practice, and tools that didn't fit neatly into the consult room. Some are useful, some are just thinking out loud in code.

For now, the longform essays still live in <a href="/field-notes/">field notes</a>. New experiments will land here.
