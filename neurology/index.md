The neurology pillar covers presentations that cross GP doorsteps regularly: headache and migraine, epilepsy, stroke prevention, tinnitus, tremor, dizziness and vertigo, and peripheral nerve conditions. The focus is on the bedside reasoning that decides whether to manage in primary care, refer urgently, or watch and wait.
